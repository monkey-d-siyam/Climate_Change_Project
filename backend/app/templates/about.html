About Climate Explorer

Empowering insights into climate change with visuals, data, and tools.

What is Climate Explorer?

Climate Explorer is a cutting-edge platform designed to help individuals, organizations, and governments better understand the impacts of climate change through interactive maps, insightful visualizations, and data-driven storytelling. Inspired by global efforts like NASA's Earth Science program, our mission is to make climate data accessible, engaging, and actionable.
